About Us

Can you remember when you met the friends you currently have? All of them? Well, we can’t either, really. Together for some time and inevitably together for some time more, the Fayette DnD group is currently just a bunch of nerds who try and get together on set days to play Dungeons and Dragons.

Aside from the occasional brutal murder, our campaigns go smoothly thanks to our fantastic DMs and members who try to make each session fun.

Not only does the Fayette DnD group run a normal 5e campaign, but we’re also working on putting together a small campaign based off Bleach in an attempt to shake up the norm and try to pull something different into our otherwise basic campaign runs. This group meets on Mondays and Wednesdays from 12 to 3 and sometimes possibly later.

Together, we share jokes, complain about classes, drive each other insane, pickpocket corpses in the middle of battles, throw rotten tomatoes, cartwheel out of the way of said tomatoes, kill our teammates, shoot our teammates, blame all of these shots on “bad aim”, inevitably all kill one another, restart campaigns, miss meetings, wonder where the fuck everyone else is, but most importantly, have fun.

Stupid, entertaining, and actually quite serious at times, the Fayette DnD group is the only group you’ll ever need to join.
